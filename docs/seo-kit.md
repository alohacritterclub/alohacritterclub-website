## 3. Image alt text

**Where:** Editor → click image → Settings (gear) → Alt Text field.

- **Venmo QR code:** Donate to Aloha Critter Club via Venmo
- **Logo:** Aloha Critter Club logo — Hawaii animal welfare nonprofit
- **Student/club photo template:** Aloha Critter Club students [what they're doing] at [school name]
- **Board/team photo:** Aloha Critter Club board members in Hawaii

Rule: one sentence, describe what's in the image, include "Aloha Critter Club" or a location name when it fits naturally. Don't keyword-stuff.

---

## 4. After pasting

1. Click **Publish** (top right of Wix editor).
2. Wix Dashboard → Marketing & SEO → SEO → run **Site Inspection** and accept the Google verification prompt.
3. In Google Search Console, add property `alohacritterclub.org` and submit `https://www.alohacritterclub.org/sitemap.xml`.

Indexing typically begins within 1–3 weeks.
